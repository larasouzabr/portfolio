32nd Workshop on Computer Education (WEI) Paper Presentation - BRASÍLIA, DF - 2024

The 32nd Workshop on Computer Education (WEI 2024) is an annual event promoted by the Brazilian Computing Society (SBC). It serves as a forum for discussions on various topics related to Computer Education. This workshop is part of the Congress of the Brazilian Computing Society (CSBC) and is organized by SBC's Education and Basic Education Commissions. At this event, we presented the paper "TERTS: A Teaching Tutor for Test Smells Refactoring", which introduces an educational tool designed to help students understand and refactor issues in software testing using Java.
